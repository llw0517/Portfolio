About Me!

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Oscar Lam, a student at the University of Waterloo. Originally from Hong Kong, I started my journey in 2022. I'm studying Financial Analysis and Risk Management, with plans for a Statistics major and Computer Science minor.

Before coming to Canada, I attended Munsang College in Hong Kong and continued my education at Milliken Mills High School. In my free time, I enjoy playing volleyball, soccer, and basketball. These sports help me stay active and teach me the value of teamwork.

I'm dedicated to achieving excellence in everything I do. Whether it's academics or personal goals, I prioritize self-motivation, collaboration, and attention to detail. My short-term goals include maintaining a GPA of 4.0 and preparing for the CFA exam.

Looking ahead, I aim to complete both the CFA and FRA exams, as well as earn a master's degree within the next 5 to 10 years. Ultimately, I want to specialize in quantitative risk analysis and make a meaningful impact in the financial world.
